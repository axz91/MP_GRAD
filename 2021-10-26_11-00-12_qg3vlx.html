What are the main factors that will make my undergraduate application to be strong, if I doing medical physics job shadowing and taking course about Radiation Physics is that will give more chance to accept in the graduate medical physics programs?

>

GPA and LORs, and maybe some physics research will matter way more than shadowing. If your shadowing can lead to a LOR then that's good, but it will carry a lot more weight if it can refer to substantial work the recommender saw you do, like research, which is obviously a longer term commitment. The value of shadowing is really just for you personally understanding the field a little better, not for adcoms.
